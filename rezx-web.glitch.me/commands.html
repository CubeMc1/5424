Rezx Bot

This unique bot gives you the freedom to manage your server, backup it in case of hack,
run giveaways, play music, with economy system.




Commands

Public
.ascii: Create text in ascii way. .avatar: Shows your avatar image.
.icon: Display server's icon/avatar. .invites: Shows how many people you/person invited.
.createinvite: Create invite link with customized options. .translate: Translates your sentence to any language.
.user: Shows user's information. .weather: Shows that latest weather status in your area.
.stats: Shows the bot's information. .emojis: Shows server's emojis.
.rep: Give a reputation point to a certain user.. .server: Display server's information.
Admin
.autorole: Shows all autorole commands. .ban: Bans a specific user.
.role: Give a role to a specific user. .kick: Kicks a specific user.
.roleremove: Remove a role from a specific user. .mute: Mutes a specific user.
.banid: Bans a user with his ID. .unmute: Removes the mute from a muted user.
.backup: Shows backup commands. .topic: Changes room's topic.
.lock: Locks the channel. .unlock: Unlocks the channel.
.warn: Warns a user for a specific reason. .warns: Views other users warns.
Tickets
.setrole: Set the support role for tickets. .new: Create a new ticket.
.add: Add a user to a specific ticket. .close: Kicks a specific user.
.remove: Remove a user from a specific ticket. .forceclose: Mutes a specific user.
.rename: Changes a specific ticket's name. .setcategory: Sets the ticket's category.
Music
.skip: Skips to the next song. .play: Plays a youtube song or playlist.
.queue: Shows the music queue. .stop: Stops the music and leave the voice channel.
.np: Shows what is playing now. .pause: Stops the song for short time.
.repeat: Repeats the song that is playing now. .resume: Continue playing from when songs got paused.
.cq: Clears all the queue. .volume: Changes the volume of the songs.
Economy
.coins: Show how much coins you have.
.claim: Collect your daily gift.
.pay: Pay a user a certain amount of coins.
Giveaways
.greroll: Rerolls a new winner. .gcreate: Creates a giveaway in the old way.
.gend: Ends a running giveaway. .gstart: Creates a giveaway.
